Welcome to Shepherdle, the rules are very simple! Your goal is to determine the doctor in 5 guesses.

As you guess you will gain information about your guess doctor as they relate to the goal:

Name Column:
If your guess shares a name or is related by blood with the goal it will be green.
If they have been in some sort of a romantic relationship it will be yellow.
And if there is no relationship it will be red.

Seasons Column:
If your guess shares a majority of its seasons with the goal character it will be green.
If they share some seasons but not most it will be yellow.
If they share none it will be red.

Specialty Column:
If your guess shares their specialty with the goal character the seasons column will be green.
If they have a related specialty IE peds->OB it will be yellow.
And as you might guess if they aren't alike it will be red.
